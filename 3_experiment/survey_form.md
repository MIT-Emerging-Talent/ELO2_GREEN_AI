# Open-Source vs. Commercial AI: Comparing Performance and Quality

Welcome and thank you for participating in this research study!

This study investigates a critical question:
"Can open-source language models, enhanced with optimization techniques such as
recursive editing and distillation, become environmentally and functionally viable
alternatives to commercial models?"

- Read each text carefully

- Rate each text on clarity, relevance, accuracy, and trustworthiness

## YOUR ROLE

Your honest evaluations will help us understand:

- Whether open-source models can achieve comparable quality to commercial alternatives
- How users perceive differences (or similarities) between the two types
- The viability of more sustainable and accessible AI solutions
